四、注意事项与应急事件处理方案

蒙古包脏乱差

  1. 务必提前联系酒店，提前打扫清洁
  2. 如果实在太脏客户不满，首先表达歉意，谴责旅馆，安抚情绪，告知情况，然后听主领队指挥重新安排住所

骑马

  1. 注意事项：不能带包，必须戴头盔，不能站在马背后（防止被踢）
  2. 骑马方法：拉缰绳，左缰绳左转，右缰绳右转，两根一起拉是刹车
  3. 意外事件：检查情况，紧急送医（去武川县）

食物

1.草原上食物比较差，安抚情绪
2.注意安排清真食物

KTV

  1. 部分乘客唱歌太难听：怂恿其他唱的好的人上去唱，合理抢麦，调动气氛，不要引起旅客之间的矛盾
  2. 唱歌到时间太晚：告诉游客部分人非常累，想回去休息，如果你们还想娱乐可以自己去酒吧和KTV（但一定要早起不能迟到）

酒吧

  1. 通宵：务必通知第二天集合时间，若迟到不等人
  2. 丢东西：若在酒吧遗失个人物品，帮忙联系保安等等人协调解决

博物馆

  1. 携带物品：不能带水和食物入馆，可寄存到馆内，但最好放在车上以防忘记
  2. 等待时长：如果9：30前到馆，拍合照拖延时间，安抚情绪，说是博物馆的问题

沙漠娱乐活动

1.换票：一定要提前通知可以换票，防止最后有乘客要求退票退钱的情况发生；85KG以上的游客不能玩滑索，可以找领队换其他票；个别女生不能玩过于刺激的运动，也可以找领队换票
2.时间：滑索等待时间非常很长，可以视情况而变，比如人少的话先去滑索排队；若队伍太长预计等待时间超过集合时间，可婉言劝说游客换其他的娱乐项目，如果游客不开心，要适当安抚情绪
3.缆车：坐缆车时候一定注意保管好物品，尤其是手机、队旗等重要物品，掉到沙漠基本找不回来，提前通知大家装到密封的袋子或包里
4.准备物资：沙漠非常干燥，通知乘客务必带1~2瓶水，最好戴好口罩做好防护
5.领队与副领队配合：一前一后看好人数，不要让乘客走丢

司机相关

  1. 车故障：可通过美团APP或其他途径及时联系上门修车的商家，若无大碍（如车门气压不够关不上）可暂时采用司机的解决办法（钢丝捆上门）
  2. 违反交通规则：提前了解好基本的交通规则，例如限高，在北京和内蒙两地区有一定差别；入京路上有一段路会有交警上车检查，提醒乘客务必系好安全带；八达岭高速公路，连续下坡，事故多发，大车的刹车容易失灵，提醒司机注意安全，提醒乘客重新上高速务必系安全带；
  3. 走错路：随时关注导航动向，另外第二天从草原出发去沙漠的路告诉司机最好走国道而非高速，减少堵车可能；
  4. .观察情绪波动：吃饭和安排住宿的时候一定得先考虑司机的感受，先把司机的饭桌和住宿安排好了，别让司机问他应该坐哪里…最好把司机都安排在一起，有共同语言，领队最后再吃饭。

自我保护相关

  1. 乘客有好感：直接说自己有男朋友，委婉拒绝一起吃饭和喝咖啡等一系列邀请。
  2. 被骚扰：说自己有男朋友还不管用，以及被莫名其妙抱了的情况下，首先冷静下来，好好讲道理，提防着这个人，如果不听就报警。
  3. 单独来的女生，尽量安排到和女生住一起，另外晚上小心喝水被下药，一定反锁好门，小心房门被打开。

五、个人意见建议

  1. 博物馆参观时间不够，仅1.5小时，建议延长到3小时，避免乘客观展不尽兴。

  2. 博物馆因为馆内有较多部分的展览没有英文介绍，外国人难以理解，需要提前告知旅客博物馆的前台可约英文讲解，10-20人一组， 可以让游客自费。

  3. 部分乘客反馈吃的不好吃，在草原时最好有勺子（有些外国人不会用筷子），另外希望每餐之前能提前通知具体吃什么类型的食物（素食/鱼/火锅），具体到早餐、午餐、晚餐，让乘客有心理准备。

  4. 沙漠之后到饺子馆时间还是太长了（两小时），乘客饿着肚子将近8点才能吃上晚饭，KTV预留时间也不多，玩的不够尽兴。因此建议是否有条件换一家更近一些的餐馆，网红水煮鱼评价比较一般，KTV评价相对更高。

重点展厅 Key Exihibitions

1、远古世界 The Acient World

l Overview: This is an exhibition about Inner Mongolia’s natural fossils and environment in ancient time, which shows the huge change of marvelous ancient ecological environment in Inner Mongolia from three billion to ten thousand years ago, especially Mesozoic and tertiary, quaternary mammals dinosaurs fossil specimens.

l 概览：主要为远古时期内蒙古天然化石和生态环境展览，展示了内蒙古从30亿年前到1万年前古代生态环境发生的巨大变迁，尤其突出中生代恐龙和第三纪、第四纪哺乳动物化石标本。

Unit 1 漫长曲折的生命历程 The Long and Tortuous Course of Life

这部分通过讲述五个故事，概括了生命的起源进化和生态环境的巨大变迁。生命在海洋中繁衍、脊椎动物的起源、四足动物的出现、生物登上陆地和生物飞向蓝天。

This unit summarizes the origin and evolution of life as well as the great changes of ecological environment by telling 5 stories: Life Multiplied in Oceans, The Origin of Vertebrates, The Emergence of Quadrupeds, Animals Got on to Land (Left the Oceans) and Animals Flew to Sky.

例如，叠层石（Stromatolite）是微生物如蓝藻的生命活动引起的矿物沉淀，是内蒙古最早的生命记录，那曾是生命最孤单的海洋时代。For example, the stromatolite is a mineral deposit caused by the life activities of microorganisms such as cyanobacteria. This is the earliest life record in Inner Mongolia, which is also the loneliest ocean time for life.

Unit 2 千姿百态的恐龙王朝 The Various Dinosaurs World

1. 禽龙（Iguanodon），是最早被发现的恐龙，他的身体像是蜥蜴。它的前肢大拇指处长有一枚尖钉状的部分，这是用来自卫的武器。Iguanodon was the first dinosaur ever discovered. Look at the dinosaur’s body. It similar to an iguana, so it also get the name. It has nails in front of his forelegs, which are weapons to protect itself.

2. 鸭嘴龙（Hadrosaur)，有着像鸭子一样的嘴部。它的牙齿咬合方式是交错排列的，它以很粗糙的植物为食，这对牙齿磨损很大。生物学家推测它咀嚼的过程中如果牙齿被磨平了，可能还会再生出新的来代替，它一生中大约要换掉近上千颗牙齿。而且它是很少见的会游泳的恐龙。It has a mouth like ducks. You can see its teeth are staggered, because it ate rough and coarse food, which would have an adverse impact on its teeth. And scientists speculated that if their teeth were worn down, they would replace new one. They may change thousands of teeth in life. And it also special for swimming skill.

3.原角龙（Protoceratops）的头有大大的一个颈盾，这是为了保护它的脖子不容易被攻击的自卫武器。原角龙的发现也给“大陆漂移说”提供了很好的证据。地球在中生代以前陆地都是连在一起的联合古陆，我们称之为“泛大陆”，随着地球自转产生了离心力和天体引潮力，陆地便逐渐的分裂并飘移形成了现在的陆地版图。

Its head like a shield, which protect the neck from attacking. The discovery of proceratops also provides evidence for an idea called Continental Drift. Before the Mesozoic era, the earth was a united continent of land, which was called “Pangea”. As the earth’s rotation generated centrifugal force and celestial tide generating force, the land gradually divided and drifted to form the current land map.

4. 查干诺尔龙(Nurosaurus)目前亚洲白垩纪最大的恐龙，在内蒙古查干诺尔被发现。查干诺尔在蒙语中是白色湖泊的意思。它体长有26米，身高12米的高度，如果我们还原了它身体的部分，那么它的体重就能达到50吨。根据它的体型就可以推测出，它每天要吃掉将近300公斤的食物。

It is a kind of largest dinosaur of the Cretaceous period in Asia, discovered in Inner Mongolia. Chaganore means white lake in Mongolian. It’s 26 meters long and 12 meters high. If we restore its body parts, its weight will reach about 50-60 tons. Based on his size, it eats an estimated 300 kilograms of food per day.

Unit 3 生机盎然的兽类世界 The Vibrant Mammals World

广为流传的小行星撞地球假说：有一颗小行星撞上地球，尘土遮天蔽日数年，植物因为没有阳光而死去，所以很多恐龙饿死了。当食物链断裂了，恐龙就慢慢灭绝了。而在恐龙灭绝之后，哺乳动物成为世界的主宰者。The popular asteroid impact hypothesis holds that after an asteroid hit earth, dust blocked out the sun for years. Plants died because there was no sunlight, so many dinosaurs no vegetable to eat and starved to death. The food chain broke down, and the dinosaurs died out. After the extinction of dinosaurs, mammals became the rulers of the world.

5.沙拉木伦始巨犀（Saramuran Rhinocero)。史上最大的陆地哺乳动物之一的始巨犀，它成年后的肩高能达到5米，体长能达到7米，是古老而巨大的奇蹄类。As one of the largest land mammals of all time, it has grown to a height of 5 meters with a body length of 7 meters. It’s an ancient and huge ungulate mammals.

6.松花江猛犸象（Songhua river mammoth)。人们在中国的东北发现了三具猛犸象的化石，其中体型最大的就是这只在松花江发现的。它也是我国目前最大的一具猛犸象化石。有些猛犸象的尸体嘴边还有青草。后来经过研究发现它皮下的脂肪都能达到9厘米厚，极度抗寒冷天气。由于它们在气候逐渐转暖时它由于无法适应环境，所以被自然界所淘汰了。

Three mammoth fossils have been found in northeast China, and the largest one of which like this was found in the Songhua River. It is also the largest mammoth fossil in China. Frozen mammoth carcasses have been found in the Siberian tundra. Some of the bodies had grass stuck to their mouths. Scientists found that they are extremely afraid of cold for fat under skin reach to 9cm. Perhaps that’s why they couldn’t adapt when the climate got warmer, and they were gradually eliminated by nature.

2、大辽契丹 The Great Liao of Khitan

契丹族是最著名的中国古代的游牧民族之一，他们有许多传奇的故事。

The Khitan people are one of the most famous nomadic people in ancient China.

政治：契丹族人民在历史上建立了强大的辽国，建立了5个都城，与宋朝对抗，统治历史达到两百多年（907-1125 AD）。

Politics: The Khitan people established a great Liao. And had five capital cities to confront the Song. Khitan ruled for Liao Dynasty more than 200 years (907-1125 AD).

l 军事：契丹族武士非常勇敢，善战且善于骑马。契丹侍卫用的兵器是“骨朵”：它非常特别，头部像南瓜，中间是棍子，用的是铁或木头做的。

Military: The Khitan warriors were brave and skillful in battles, and they were good at horsing. The weapon used by the Khitan guards was “Gu duo”: it was very special, with a head like a pumpkin and a stick in the middle, which made by iron or wood.

l 制度：按照四时捺钵表，他们顺应自然和皇帝，遵守相对固定的四季制度和活动。

Regimen: Using the List of Nabo in Four Seasons, which means they complied with nature and the emperor, and abided by the relatively fixed system and activities of the four seasons.

春：出门 捕鹅抓鱼 夏：热 不出门 讨论政务

秋：出门 射鹿 冬：冷 不出门 讨论政务

Spring: hunt geese and catch fish Summer: (too hot) stay home and discuss affairs

Fall: shoot deer to eat Winter: (too cold) stay home and discuss affairs

Notes: affairs include summoning the leaders of the other clans and talk about cooperation.

不同季节不同活动让他们的艺术也呈现不同风格。例如春水玉（两种鹅），秋山玉（鹿、山林和老虎的元素）。

Different activities in different seasons make their art showed different styles. For example, Chunshui Jade (two kinds of geese) and Qiushan jade (elements of deer, mountain forest and tiger) are good examples.

l 艺术：包括陶瓷、铜器、音乐、舞蹈和纹饰（龙凤鸳鸯）。从这里可以看出他们被汉人影响了很多。

Art: These include ceramics, bronze ware, music, dancing and ornamentation (such as Dragon & Phoenix and Mandarin Duck), which shows how they were influenced deeply by the Han Chinese.

宗教：主要是萨满教。随着疆土扩大，国家管理难度加大，辽朝皇帝倡导儒教佛教等各种宗教并行发展。再后来佛教兴盛，契丹社会兴建了许多佛寺和庙宇，例如山西大同应县木塔和华严宝塔。（就像眼前的这个模型，有地宫的七层宝塔，可以让我们爬上去。另外推荐大家可以游览山西大同古城，那边许多建筑印证这段历史。）

Religion: Mostly Shamanism. However, with the expansion of territory, the difficulty of state administration increased, and the emperor advocated the parallel development of Confucianism, Buddhism and other religions. Then, Buddhism flourished, and many Buddhist temples and temples were constructed in the Khitan society, such as the wooden pagoda and Huayan pagoda in Yingxian county, Datong county, Shanxi Province.

(Notes: Just like this model here, there are seven story pagodas in the basement, which we can climb up. In addition, we recommend that you can visit the Datong City in Shanxi, where many buildings witness this history.)

l 消失：两百年后辽朝官员腐败国力衰落，大辽国被金朝灭亡，契丹族流落四处消失不知去向。历史学家推测他们很可能融入到了其他民族中。中国有很多文学作品讲述了契丹族故事，例如著名的金庸小说天龙八部讲述的就是大辽契丹和宋朝汉人相爱相杀的故事。

Disappear: 200 years later, the Liao official corruption and the decline of national strength, the collapse of the great Liao by the Jin dynasty, the Khitan people everywhere disappeared. Now historians speculate that they probably blended in with other peoples. There are many literary works in China that tell the stories of the Khitan people. For example, the famous Chinese novel Demi-Gods and Semi-Devils written by Jin Yong tells a hero’s story that time.

3**.** 天骄蒙古 The P**rou**d Son of Heaven

It displays Mongols’ history and culture, such as Genghis Khan and the Yuan dynasty. From the Mongolian origin to Yuan dynasty to the Ming and Qing dynasties complete context of nearly one thousand years, Mongolian created an intelligence, brilliant creation and magnificent history.

该展厅是古代蒙古历史陈列，例如成吉思汗和元朝历史。从元代蒙古起源到明清时期完成近一千年，蒙古人的智慧创造了一段创建和宏伟的历史。

Unit 1 熔铁出山 民族初兴 The Emergence of the Mongols

The Mongolian nationality originates from Silik river. The Mongolian people live on the grassland and make a living from animal husbandry. Living a nomadic life, although this way of living has been weakened in modern society, it is still regarded as a symbol of the Mongolian people.

蒙古族始源于古代望建河（今额尔古纳河）东岸一带。蒙古族人民世居草原，以畜牧为生计。过着“逐水草而居”的游牧生活，尽管这种生存方式在现代社会被弱化，但仍然被视作蒙古族的标志。

Unit 2 逐鹿群雄 汗国崛起 Rise of The Great Mongol Nation

Tiemuzhen began to unify the Mongolian plateau in the middle of the 12th century. At the beginning of the 13th century, he united the Mongol tribes and gradually formed a new national community. And he also owed the name Genghis Khan. He created the writing system, the army, the officers and so on, which lead to a country system. He constantly launched aggressive wars to expand his territory, and the invasion looms even over eastern Europe, which made the Mongols became famous and went to the world.

铁木真从12世纪中叶开始统一蒙古高原的事业。13世纪初，以他为首的蒙古部统一了蒙古地区诸部，逐渐形成了一个新的民族共同体，他也被尊称为成吉思汗。他创建文字、组建军队、设置官员等措施创建了完整的国家制度。他南征北战开阔疆土，甚至侵略逼近东欧，这些让蒙古民族成名并走向了世界。

Unit 3 大元盛世 多元共融 Glory of The Yuan Dinasty

The yuan dynasty, founded by the Mongol nationality, was the first unified dynasty established by the ethnic minorities in Chinese history. The capital of Dadu (now Beijing), handed down to the fifth and eleventh emperors, lasted 98 years.

元朝由蒙古族建立，是中国历史上首次由少数民族建立的大一统王朝。定都大都（今北京），传五世十一帝，历时九十八年。

Unit 4 明清蒙古 绵续繁衍 Mongols in The Ming and Qing Dynasty

After the collapse of the yuan dynasty, the remaining forces retreated to the Mongolian grasslands. The Ming emperor set up more than 20 Mongol Wei Suo (leader in areas), each chief is held by the feudal Lord of Mongolia. At the beginning of the 15th century, the Mongolians had to pay tribute to the Ming dynasty, and established a relationship of subordination.

元朝灭亡以后，残余力量退居蒙古草原。明朝皇帝设置了蒙古卫所20多个，各卫所长官都是由蒙古封建领主担任。15世纪初，蒙古族需要向明朝称臣纳贡，建立了臣属关系。

Unit 5 游牧风韵 天人合一 Nomadic Life and Religious Beliefs

The Mongolians in the Qing dynasty still kept livestock such as horses and sheep as their main business. The nomadic Mongolians are more characteristic of grassland in terms of clothing, food, shelter and transportation. They have many pioneering creations, such as the Mongolian women’s clothing. The Qing dynasty introduced Tibetan Buddhism to Mongolia.

清朝的蒙古族仍然以畜牧业经济为主业，饲养马和羊等牲畜。游牧的蒙古族在衣食住行等方面更具有草原特色。他们有许多开拓性的创造，例如蒙古族妇女的衣着服饰。清朝向蒙古推行藏传佛教，因此在蒙古地区寺庙林立，僧侣众多。
